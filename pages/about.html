This is me

Ever since I was an eleven year old on his mom’s laptop, I’ve been making my own games. A book on programming, a few rainy days in England and an old office computer shaped the beginning of my passion. A passion that only grows every day.

Since then, I’ve specialized in game development, web design and development, compliers and programming languages. I am fluent in C++ (11 and up mostly), C#, Java and Javascript (Typescript). I’m always just a few lines of code away from making anything I — or you — could want.

Aside from code, I’m also fluent in music. Whether it’s listening, playing, composing or producing, I find time for music almost every day. What I also do as often as I can, is sports. I’ve done speedskating competatively since I was little and performed on a high level in the dutch speedskating scene. Because of my studies I no longer compete on the same level, but I’ve started training pupils and have started bouldering (rock climbing) three times a week, which I've been doing ever since.
